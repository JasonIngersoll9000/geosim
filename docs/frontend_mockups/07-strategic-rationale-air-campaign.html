STRATEGIC RATIONALE //
INTENSIFY AIR CAMPAIGN: SECTOR 7

Pre-emptive Denial of Strategic Reinforcement

REF: DOC_RA_772

The current analytical model suggests that the adversary's operational window is contingent upon the unhindered flow of heavy armor and logistical support through the T-9 Corridor. By intensifying the air campaign within Sector 7, we initiate a systematic degradation of critical infrastructure that serves as the primary conduit for these assets.

Targeting primary logistics hubs—specifically the K-Node Rail Interchanges and Sub-surface Fuel Reservoirs—disrupts the adversary's ability to mobilize effectively. Without these nodes, the transition from mobilization to active projection is halted. This creates a "logistics bottleneck" that forces the adversary into a reactive posture, effectively neutralizing their initiative before it can manifest on the front line.
